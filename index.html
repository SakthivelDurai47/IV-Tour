🌄 Industrial Visit to Vagamon

Overview

Get ready for an exciting Industrial Visit to the breathtaking hill station of Vagamon, organized by the 3rd Year CSE Department. This two-day getaway is designed to offer a perfect blend of nature, learning, and unforgettable memories with friends.

  • 📍 Destination: Thiruvananthapuram & Vagamon
  • ⏳ Duration: 2 Days / 2 Night
  • 🚌 Departure: TEC, Tirunelveli
  • 💰 Estimated Cost: ₹ 3,500
  • 📅 Date: 1st week of september
Trip Highlights
  • 🛍️ Shopping & Leisure at Lulu Mall
  • 🏖️ Beach Visit for Relaxation
  • 🌿 Off-road Jeep Safari to Idukki Forest View, Idukki Dam View, Uluppuni Tunnel, Uluppuni Waterfalls & Suicide Point
  • 🌾 Scenic Walk through Vagamon Meadows
  • 🌲 Visit to Pine Forest
  • 🕌 Thangal Para Visit

* All arrangements will be handled by the organizing team including travel, food, stays and all safety procedures

Trip Plan

🌄 Industrial Visit – Vagamon Trip

Organized by: Department of CSE

Duration: 2 Days / 2 Night

Pickup Location: Thamirabharani Engineering College, Tirunelveli

Destination: Thiruvananthapuram & Vagamon

🚌 Day 1: Departure & Coastal Leisure

   ⏰ 11:00 PM

Departure from College: Attendance check, luggage loading, and seat allocation. Board the AC tourist bus for a comfortable overnight journey towards Thiruvananthapuram.

   🌅 07:30 AM

Resort stop: Get freshened up at the resort and get ready for the day, breakfast will be served.

   🏭 11:00 AM

Industrial Visit at M Squared Private Limited:
- Welcome briefing by HR/Training head.
- Introduction to company history, operations, and services.
- Session on Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) and agile methodologies.
- Networking session with engineers for Q&A.
- Industry safety and workplace conduct briefing.
Duration: ~3 hours.

   🍛 01:00 PM

Lunch: Group lunch at a nearby restaurant (pre-booked vegetarian & non-vegetarian menu).

   🛒 02:00 PM

Lulu Mall Visit: Kerala’s largest shopping mall. Short supervised leisure time for snacks, exploring retail outlets, and group pictures.

   🏖️ 04:30 PM

Beach Visit: Relax at a coastal location (Kovalam). Activities: casual walk, photography, light group games.

   🚐 06:00 PM

Drive to Vagamon: Scenic hillside bus ride to the pre-booked resort. Duration: ~5 hours including tea break.

   🍽️ 09:30 PM

Dinner & Check-in: Settle into resort rooms, freshen up, and enjoy a buffet dinner. Overnight stay and campfire activities.

🌞 Day 2: Jeep Safari & Hill Adventures

   🍳 07:30 AM

Breakfast: Resort buffet breakfast.

   🚙 08:00 AM

Off-Road Jeep Safari: 4×4 jeeps will take us through:
- Idukki Forest View: Misty trails, photography spots, and short bird-watching stops.
- Idukki Dam View: Panoramic stop with guide talk on dam structure and history.
- Uluppuni Tunnel: Quick walk-through of a natural tunnel-like path.
- Uluppuni Waterfalls: Short trek to a serene waterfall.
- Suicide Point: Cliff-top viewpoint for dramatic photos.
Safari duration: ~4 hours.

   🍲 12:30 PM

Lunch: Group lunch

   🚌 02:00 PM

Scenic Spots by Bus:
- Vagamon Meadows: Rolling green hills ideal for relaxation and group photography.
- Pine Forest: Tall pine trees with shaded walking trails.
- Thangal Para: Cultural spot with panoramic views.

   🚍 04:30 PM

Return Journey: Depart Vagamon towards Tirunelveli. Dinner en route at a pre-selected restaurant.

   ⏰ 10:30 PM

Arrival at College: Unload luggage, quick headcount, and disperse.

✅ Trip Inclusions

  • AC Tourist Bus travel: Tirunelveli → Thiruvananthapuram → Vagamon → Return
  • 1 Night resort stay
  • All meals as provided
  • Jeep Safari to off-road destinations
  • Entry/industry visit arrangements and fees
  • Tour coordinators and safety assistance
  • Price: ₹3,500 per person

📌 Notes

  • • Wear comfortable clothing and trekking shoes.
  • • Carry personal medication, and a refillable water bottle.
  • • Bring valid ID proof (id card) for entry/industry visits.
  • • Follow guide instructions and respect local culture and environment.
