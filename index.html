🌄 Industrial Visit to Vagamon

Overview

Get ready for an exciting Industrial Visit to the breathtaking hill station of Vagamon, organized by the 3rd Year CSE Department. This two-day getaway is designed to offer a perfect blend of nature, learning, and unforgettable memories with friends.

  • 📍 Destination: Vagamon, Kerala
  • ⏳ Duration: 2 Days / 1 Night
  • 🚌 Departure: TEC, Tirunelveli
  • 💰 Estimated Cost: ₹ 3,500
  • 📅 Date: To be Announced
Trip Highlights
  • 🌿 Off-road Jeep Safari to scenic spots like Uluppuni Waterfalls, Suicide Point & Photo Viewpoint
  • 🌄 Kurushimala Trekking Adventure
  • 🔥 Campfire Night with DJ Beats
  • 🏭 Tea Factory Industrial Visit
  • 🌲 Visit to Pine Forest & Adventure Park
  • 🛍️ Shopping & Relaxation at Meadows Valley

* All safety and travel arrangements will be handled by the organizing team.

Trip Plan

🌄 Industrial Visit – Vagamon Trip

Organized by: Department of CSE

Duration: 2 Days / 1 Night

Pickup Location: Thamirabharani Engineering College, Tirunelveli

Destination: Vagamon, Kerala

🚌 Day 1: Departure & Adventure Begins

  • Morning – Departure from Tirunelveli: Assemble and begin the journey to Vagamon.
  • Arrival & Fresh-up: Reach destination, check-in, and freshen up.
  • Breakfast: Traditional Kerala-style breakfast.

🚙 Off-Road Jeep Safari

Explore the lush green valleys and hidden gems of Vagamon in thrilling jeep rides:

Uluppuni Waterfalls

A serene hidden cascade amidst lush greenery.

Uluppuni Tunnel

A mysterious, abandoned tunnel perfect for exploration.

Idukkidam Viewpoint

Breathtaking panoramic views of the Western Ghats.

Lemongrass Valley

Walk through valleys filled with the scent of fresh lemongrass.

Photo Point

The perfect backdrop for Instagram-worthy shots.

Suicide Point

Dramatic cliffs offering stunning yet thrilling views.

Lunch: Kerala-style lunch at a local restaurant or campsite.

🥾 Kurushimala Trekking

An energizing trek to Kurushimala – spiritual and scenic viewpoint.

🔥 Night Activities

  • Campfire with DJ Music – Music, dance, and celebration
  • Dinner – Delicious Kerala cuisine
  • Night Stay – Safe and clean accommodation in Vagamon

🌞 Day 2: Nature + Learning + Fun

  • Breakfast: Refreshing morning breakfast
  • Tea Factory Industrial Visit: Educational tour of tea processing and production

Learn how tea is processed and packaged right from the source.

  • Pine Forest: Nature walk through Vagamon's iconic pine woods
  • Adventure Park: Optional activities like ziplining, rope climbing (subject to time)
  • Lunch: Full meal before the final journey
  • Meadows Valley: Scenic hills, ideal for photography and relaxation
  • Shopping: Souvenirs, spices, local snacks, and tea

🚍 Return Journey

Depart Vagamon and return to Tirunelveli by night.

✅ Trip Inclusions:

  • Round-trip transportation from College
  • 1 Night Accommodation
  • All meals (2 Breakfasts, 2 Lunches, 1 Dinner)
  • Off-Road Jeep Safari
  • Trek guide and equipment
  • Campfire and DJ Night
  • Entry fees and industrial visit access
  • Tour coordinators and safety assistance

📌 Note:

  • • Carry comfortable clothing, trekking shoes, and personal medication
  • • Bring valid ID proof
  • • Respect local culture and maintain cleanliness
